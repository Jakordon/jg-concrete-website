About JG Concrete

Local, hands-on, and built on a real concrete education.

CIM Graduate — Texas State

Hi, I'm Jacob

I'm a concrete contractor based in East Dallas, and concrete has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. I grew up around the trade, watching family members work, learning the basics early, and developing a real respect for the craft. That background eventually led me to the Concrete Industry Management program at Texas State University, where I learned the technical side of concrete — mix design, materials testing, quality control, and how to build work that lasts. Combining what I learned at home with what I learned in the CIM program shaped the way I approach every project today.

Along with my education, I've built hands-on experience completing patios, driveways, walkways, small slabs, and repair work throughout the Dallas area. I take pride in showing up, communicating clearly, and treating every project like it's going in my own yard. I'm growing my portfolio one job at a time, focusing on clean finishes, solid prep, and dependable results. When you hire me, you're getting someone who genuinely cares about the work and the people he's building for.
